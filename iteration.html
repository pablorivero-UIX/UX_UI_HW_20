Competitive Analysis

Comparing our ideas with our direct or indirect competitors may lead us to iterate upon our original features before starting designing the prototypes.

Guerrilla User Testing

We ideate a plan to test our initial prototype. It is a low-cost method to evaluate how effective an interface is, and find out usability problems with common tasks such as: online shopping, completing a form or the onboarding process..

A/B Testing

This type of test compares two alternatives for a interface design. The user would give feedback and we can also measure the effectiveness of elements such us CTA buttons or the
navigation system.
