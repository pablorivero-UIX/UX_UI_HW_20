Low Fidelity Prototypes

In this stage we design our app's prototype screens focusing on our main UI elements and navigation; to do so we avoid using distracting elements like images and colour.

Iteration & Conventions

In this phase we carry out some modifications based on our low-fi prototype tests and we apply design conventions depending on which design system is going to be display in: Apple or Android.

High-Fidelity Protoype
